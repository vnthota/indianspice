  • Indian Spice

    Indian Spice, a paradise for Indian food is strategically located near the ‘Kun Iam Statue’ in Macau, within walking distance from Wynn Macau, MGM Grand and Sands Macau.

    With outdoor seating also provided, every need of the guests has been taken into consideration. For people, whose spirits go up, when the spirits go down, all types of quality wines, beers and hard drinks are also available in our well-stocked bar.

    Indian Spice provides a taste-bud tickling range of North Indian recipes. We also cater to Jain and Gujarati food tastes, with a mouth-watering range of vegetarian, non-vegetarian and tandoori dishes. Indian Spice can cater to meal requests from tourists, from different parts of India.

  • Machan India

    Machan Restaurant is a beautifully landscaped, fine-dining restaurant, with a unique ambience, strategically located opposite the Nagpur Airport, on the busy Wardha Road.

    Machan specialises in Indian, Chinese and Tandoori cuisines. Machan is popular with families, for its ambience, and food quality, and also because it does not serve alcohol.

    Machan has its set of dedicated clientele. It offers a variety of seating options, like the garden, on or below the Machan, inside the water-body, and in the Air-conditioned Hall.
